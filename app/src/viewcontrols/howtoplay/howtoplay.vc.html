OVERVIEW OF GAMEPLAY


  • 1
  • Pick Your Contest

CoverSharks is different because it lets you choose your contest and how you want to play. Enter the Shark Tank and choose college teams to play against the point spread or the total in our weekly games.

  • 2
  • Choose How You Want To Play

Do you want to compete alongside your favorite teams? Do you follow the entire nation or a specific conference or region of the country? CoverSharks gives you the option to customize your play with our unique format of games that suits your personal preferences.

Score Points and Win

Once the game begins, teams and players compete to win points. Woth each win, you can accumulate points that can be redeemed.

  • 3
  • Play For Free

CoverSharks lets you play for free in any of our games. Want to play with more on the line than picking the winner? Choose from a variety of games and choose your competition each and every week of the season.

  • 4
  • Play with Friends, Family, Rivals & Co-workers

Once you see for yourself how much fun you will have, play with everyone from friends to enemies for the ultimate game experience. Play head-to-head, in an open league or create your own custom league to see who has bragging rights in real time with every game.
